The process

Four steps, zero guesswork

01
Capture

Photograph the vehicle on-site

Open CarSee on your phone and take photos of the vehicle from any angle. The app guides you through capturing each panel so nothing gets missed — no training required, no special equipment, just your camera.

Any angle Multiple panels Works offline
02
Detection

Three detection models run simultaneously

The moment you submit, three independent damage detection models analyse every image in parallel. Each one looks for different damage types — dents, scratches, cracks, deformations — and the results are cross-referenced to produce a single, high-confidence damage map.

Parallel processing Cross-referenced results On-device inference
03
Costing

Repair costs matched to your local market

Damage findings are priced using a repair cost database built on what workshops in your region actually charge — not averaged global figures that bear no relation to the invoice you'll receive. Parts, labour, and paint are itemised separately.

04
Report

A complete report, ready to file

CarSee generates a professional PDF report in Arabic and English — with damage photos, descriptions, part-by-part cost estimates, and your company branding. Attach it to the claim, send it to the client, or export the data to Excel. Done.
