Affiliate Sales Representative

Commission-based · Freelance · GCC / MENA

About the role

CarSee is an AI-powered vehicle damage assessment platform built for the GCC market. We help insurance companies, fleet managers, rental agencies, and dealerships assess damage and estimate repair costs — faster, more accurately, and in Arabic and English. We are looking for motivated sales affiliates who can bring CarSee to the businesses that need it most.

What you will do

  • Identify and approach prospective clients in the insurance, fleet management, car rental, and automotive dealership sectors across the GCC and broader MENA region.
  • Present CarSee's capabilities to decision-makers and purchasing teams, clearly communicating the platform's value in the context of each prospect's operations.
  • Guide prospects through the onboarding process and act as their primary point of contact during the initial engagement phase.
  • Build and maintain long-term relationships with clients, ensuring continued satisfaction and identifying opportunities for account growth.
  • Share market feedback and insights with the CarSee team to support product and business development.

What we are looking for

  • Demonstrated experience in B2B sales, business development, or client acquisition, preferably within the automotive, insurance, or technology sectors.
  • An existing professional network in the GCC or MENA region that is relevant to our target industries.
  • Strong communication and negotiation skills in Arabic. Proficiency in English is an advantage.
  • Self-directed and results-oriented, with the ability to manage your own pipeline without close supervision.
  • Comfortable presenting and demonstrating a software product to professional audiences, including procurement and operations teams.

Compensation

This is a commission-based freelance arrangement. Earnings are directly tied to the accounts you bring on board, with no ceiling on potential income. Full details of the commission structure will be shared with shortlisted candidates.

How to apply

Send your CV and a brief note on your relevant background and market reach to:

carsee.ai@gmail.com

Please use the subject line: Affiliate Application

CarSee is committed to building a team that reflects the diversity of the markets we serve. All qualified applicants will be considered regardless of background.
