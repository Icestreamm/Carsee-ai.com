Delete My Account

If you use the CarSee mobile app or have a CarSee account, you can request that we delete your account and the personal data associated with it. This page explains how to submit that request.

How to request account deletion

Send an email from the same email address you used to register your CarSee account:

To: carsee.ai@gmail.com

Subject: Delete my account

Message: Delete my account

You may add any details that help us verify your account (for example, your full name or the phone number linked to the account).

Open email to request deletion

What we delete

After we verify your request, we delete or anonymize, where applicable:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Contact information associated with your account (email, phone number, name)
  • Inspection history, vehicle photos, and reports stored under your account in the CarSee app
  • App usage and preferences tied to your user ID

What may be retained

We may keep limited information only where required by law, to resolve disputes, enforce our terms, or prevent fraud. Any such data is retained only for the period required and is not used for marketing.

Processing time

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and associated data have been deleted.

Questions

For privacy-related questions, see our Privacy Policy or contact us at carsee.ai@gmail.com.
