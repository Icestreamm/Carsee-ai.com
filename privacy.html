Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

CarSee collects and processes personal and vehicle-related data to deliver AI-powered vehicle inspection and assessment services. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

2. Data We Collect

We collect: vehicle photos and related imagery; damage and condition assessment data generated by our AI; usage data (e.g. how the service is used); and account and contact information when you sign up or get in touch.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use data to generate inspection and assessment reports, to improve the accuracy of our AI models, and to deliver and maintain our services. We may use aggregated, anonymized data for research and product improvement.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in secure, encrypted cloud environments. We apply industry-standard measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration and comply with applicable regional data protection requirements.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only with authorized partners or service providers necessary to operate our services, under strict confidentiality and data processing agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or request portability of your personal data where applicable by law. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Contact

For privacy-related requests: privacy@carsee.app.
