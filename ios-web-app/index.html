Install CarSee on your iPhone

CarSee runs as a secure web app at app.carsee-ai.com. Add it to your Home Screen for quick, full-screen access — the same inspection workflow, optimized for iOS.

Open CarSee Web App ← Back to Our App

Use Safari on iPhone or iPad. “Add to Home Screen” is available in Safari only. If you opened this page in Chrome, WhatsApp, or another in-app browser, copy the link and open it in Safari first.

Installation steps

1

Open Safari and visit CarSee

On your iPhone or iPad, open Safari and go to app.carsee-ai.com. Sign in with your CarSee account when prompted.

2

Tap the Share button

At the bottom of Safari (or top on iPad), tap the Share icon — a square with an arrow pointing upward.

3

Choose “Add to Home Screen”

Scroll down the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen. You can edit the name if you wish — “CarSee” is recommended.

4

Confirm and launch

Tap Add in the top corner. The CarSee icon appears on your Home Screen. Open it anytime for a full-screen app experience.

Common questions

Is this a download from the App Store?

CarSee on iOS is delivered as a web app. There is no App Store install required — you add app.carsee-ai.com to your Home Screen using Safari.

Does it work on iPad?

Yes. Follow the same steps in Safari on iPad. The Share button is at the top of the screen on most iPad models.

Can I use Chrome on iOS?

For installation, use Safari. After CarSee is on your Home Screen, always launch it from that icon for the best experience.
